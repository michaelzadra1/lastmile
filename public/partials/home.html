No matter who you are, Last Mile can save you money in the world of delivery logistics

Delivery Companies

Learn how Last Mile can save your delivery company up to 30% in transporation costs to major Canadian cities.



The “Last Mile” in logistics refers to the extra costs of the last leg of a delivery, commonly occurring when a vehicle has reached an inner city. These costs could represent up to 30% the costs of shipping, and is an area in which cost savings can be realized for any delivery company.


Our company connects your small business customers to our independant drivers, who are willing to complete this last mile of the delivery to these locations, for a discount or in store credit. Your drivers would simply unload their delivery contents at one of our many locations located outside major Canadian cities, and independent drivers would pick up the packages from there and complete your delivery.

Independent Drivers

Learn how you can earn money with Last Mile as an independant driver




Last Mile gives you the opportunity as an independent driver, to complete package deliveries to small businesses located within your city. Completing these deliveries will reward you with in-store credits, and major discounts at this business’s locations.


Using the Last Mile mobile application, you may view which business locations in your city are awaiting deliveries. Simply pick up the package from one of our many locations in your area, and realize the major savings at your favorite locations by completing the delivery.

Business Owners

Learn how Last Mile can generate more customers for your business, and increase your sales.




Last Mile gives you the opportunity to generate customers for your business, by joining the Last Mile network of businesses and retailers. Incoming deliveries are completed by independent drivers using the Last Mile mobile application. In return, these drivers are rewarded with in-store credit at your location.


The use of in-store credit has been proven to increase sales in business retailers. 61% of in-store credit holders spend more than the balance of the credit. Also, 75% of those shoppers who do overspend will spend 60% more of the value of the credit.
